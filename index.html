Indulge yourself with literary pieces by two people sharing

common love and passion for writing.

"tonight, i write"

tonight, i write
to tell you it's okay.
life has brought experiences
and people
sometimes to
confuse us,
test us,
but most importantly
to let us learn.
tonight, i write
to tell you i'm gonna be okay.
so focus fixing
what's needed to be fixed;
yourself,
failed relationship,
mental health,
your well-being.
tonight, i write
with a heart that wants
only for you to be happy.
——

it's a sacrifice she made as she chose to let the person she loves to go on his own way. at some point of our lives, we need to do it for them, right?

"whole completely"

Much was gone of the wounds of the past
for in God's perfect timing,
it will ultimately heal—
if not completely, but stop bleeding.
And though
you may not appear as you were before,
think of it as a blossoming
of the very flower that you are.
For whatever happened yesterday
remains buried, and if it resurfaces,
know that you are
whole.
Always,
always have been,
whole.
———

you are always complete despite the difficulties and heartaches thrown at you. cry, if needs be. sooner than later, you will realize that self-love is self-healing itself.
